About me

I am a Senior DevSecOps Engineer with more than 5 years of experience in automating, securing, and managing cloud-based solutions. Currently working at Infosys, my specialty lies in the integration of security in the DevOps lifecycle to ensure not just operational efficiency but also compliance. My expertise extends across a wide range of DevOps tools and best practices in areas such as Azure DevOps, GitHub Actions, Kubernetes, and Terraform, all leading to building robust, scalable infrastructure.

As I have deep knowledge of cloud technologies like Azure Functions, Logic Apps, and Dynamics 365, I lean toward the automation of development pipeline with the inclusions of security best practice in each phase of SDLC. I work on the design, implementation, and security of cloud architectures and automation of workflows and ensuing deployments in production environments. What I am passionate about currently is that building a solution that is agile and secure at the same time should and can help to streamline the process of development while safeguarding critical systems and data.
